Privacy Policy

TrekNest Bhutan ("we", "our") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. What we collect

When you create an account or book a tour with us, we collect:

  • Account info — your username, email address, name, and phone number.
  • Booking info — the tours and accommodations you book, dates, number of guests, special requests, and emergency contact details if provided.
  • Payment status — we record whether a payment has been received. We do not store credit card numbers (payments are currently arranged off-platform via bank transfer or in person).
  • Technical info — IP address and basic browser information, captured automatically in server logs for security and abuse prevention.

2. Why we collect it

We use your information only to:

  • Process and manage your bookings.
  • Communicate with you about your tour (confirmations, itinerary, last-minute changes, weather warnings).
  • Verify your account and reset your password if you forget it.
  • Comply with Bhutanese tourism regulations (the Tourism Council of Bhutan may require us to share a guest list for permitted treks).
  • Improve the website and detect abuse.

We do not sell your data to advertisers, brokers, or any third party.

3. Who has access

Within TrekNest, only the staff guide leading your tour and our administrators can see your booking details. We share your information only with:

  • Guest houses you've booked with us — they receive your name, phone number, and arrival dates so they can prepare for your stay.
  • The Tourism Council of Bhutan and Department of Immigration when legally required for permits and visa formalities.
  • Service providers we use to operate (email delivery, hosting, error monitoring), under strict data-protection terms.

4. How long we keep it

Account information is kept for as long as your account is active. Booking records are kept for 7 years after the tour date for tax, accounting, and regulatory reasons. You may request earlier deletion (see Section 6).

5. How we protect it

  • All website traffic is encrypted with HTTPS (TLS).
  • Passwords are stored as one-way hashes (we cannot read them, even if we wanted to).
  • Database access is restricted and behind a firewall.
  • Daily backups are kept and encrypted at rest.
  • Staff who can see customer data are vetted and bound by confidentiality.

6. Your rights

You may, at any time, request to:

  • See a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct anything that is inaccurate (most fields are self-serve in your My Account page).
  • Delete your account (we will keep only what is required by law).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.

Email us at the address on the Contact page and we'll respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies: an authentication token (so you stay logged in) and a small piece of browser storage that holds your current user profile. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. We may use a privacy-respecting analytics tool to count page views, but we will never sell that data.

8. Children

Our services are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. Parents who believe we hold information about a minor should contact us immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our service evolves. Significant changes will be announced on the homepage and emailed to active users. The "last updated" date is at the bottom of this page.

10. Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? See Contact.
