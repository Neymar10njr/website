About TrekNest Bhutan

A Thimphu-based tour operator specialising in guided treks, day hikes, and Buddhist pilgrimages across Bhutan.

Who we are

TrekNest Bhutan is a small, dedicated tour operator built around one belief: that the experience of trekking and pilgrimage in Bhutan should be authentic, well-organised, and led by people who know the land. We are not a marketplace listing strangers — every tour you book with us is planned by our team and led by one of our certified Bhutanese guides.

What we organise

  • Treks — multi-day journeys through Bhutan's dzongkhags, from the iconic Druk Path to the legendary Snowman Trek.
  • Day hikes — accessible single-day walks around Paro, Thimphu, and Punakha for travellers with limited time.
  • Pilgrimages — guided visits to the sacred sites of Bhutanese Buddhism, including Taktsang, the Bumthang circuit, the Punakha sacred valley, and Phobjikha.
  • Accommodation — we partner with verified local guest houses along every route so you sleep close to the mountains, not in a generic city hotel.

How a TrekNest tour works

  1. You browse our scheduled departures on the Group Tours page or pick a trek you'd like to do.
  2. You reserve your spot online. We email you a booking reference.
  3. We follow up with payment instructions (currently bank transfer or in-person on arrival).
  4. On the trek day, your lead guide meets you at the agreed meeting point with all logistics handled — permits, transport, meals, camping gear where needed.
  5. You walk. We carry the rest.

Why a single-operator model

Many trekking platforms are marketplaces of independent guides. We chose a different path: TrekNest Bhutan is the operator. Every tour is planned and led by our small team, vetted by us, with the Tourism Council of Bhutan licence we hold as a company. This means:

  • One point of accountability — if something goes wrong, you call us, not a random person.
  • Consistent quality — every group benefits from the same standard of guiding, gear, and safety.
  • Trained local guides — all our staff are TCB-certified Bhutanese guides with wilderness first-aid training.
